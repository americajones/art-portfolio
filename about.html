About Me


Twitter | GitHub

My name is America Jones and I am a student of the world! You can call me AJ.

I've been an artist my entire life, finding it much easier to communicate ideas and feelings through images over words. Creation is in my bones, whether oil painting, stamp carving or drawing neon digital works. I love colors that scream and textures that snatch. Really, what I am at heart is a storyteller.

I like to spend my free time loving my wife, walking my dog, and studying various languages. I'm a quick learner who likes to pick up all sorts of things for fun and profit(but mostly storytelling).
